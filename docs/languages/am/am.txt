============

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/amh>`__ identifies this language as ``amh``.

* DJ: Djibouti
* ER: Eritrea
* ET: Ethiopia
* DE: Germany
* US: United States of America
* CA: Canada
* EG: Egypt
