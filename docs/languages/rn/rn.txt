Ikirundi
========

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/run>`__ identifies this language as ``run``.

* KE: Kenya
* RW: Rwanda
* TZ: Tanzania, United Republic of
* CA: Canada
* UG: Uganda
* BI: Burundi
