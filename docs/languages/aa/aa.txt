Afaraf
======

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/aar>`__ identifies this language as ``aar``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* DJ: Djibouti
* ER: Eritrea
* ET: Ethiopia
* US: United States of America
* CA: Canada
