Sesotho
=======

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/sot>`_ identifies this language as ``sot``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* ZA: South Africa
* US: United States of America
* LS: Lesotho
* SZ: Eswatini
* BW: Botswana
