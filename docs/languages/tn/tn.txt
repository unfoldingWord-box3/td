Setswana
--------

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/tsn>`__ identifies this language as ``tsn``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* ZA: South Africa
* US: United States of America
* ZW: Zimbabwe
* BW: Botswana
* NA: Namibia
