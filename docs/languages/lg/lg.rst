Luganda
=======

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/lug>`_ identifies this language as ``lug``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* KE: Kenya
* TZ: Tanzania, United Republic of
* US: United States of America
* CA: Canada
* UG: Uganda
