Caribbean Hindustani
====================

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* TT: Trinidad and Tobago
* GY: Guyana
* NL: Netherlands
* SX: Sint Maarten (Dutch part)
* SR: Suriname

.. warning:: The gateway language is not valid.
