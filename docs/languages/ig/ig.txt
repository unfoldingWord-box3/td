Ásụ̀sụ̀ Ị̀gbò
========================

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/ibo>`__ identifies this language as ``ibo``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* CM: Cameroon
* GQ: Equatorial Guinea
* GH: Ghana
* NG: Nigeria
* US: United States of America
* CA: Canada
