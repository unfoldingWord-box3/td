Wollof
======

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/wol>`__ identifies this language as ``wol``.

* GA: Gabon
* ML: Mali
* MR: Mauritania
* SN: Senegal
* US: United States of America
* CA: Canada
* GW: Guinea-Bissau
* IT: Italy
