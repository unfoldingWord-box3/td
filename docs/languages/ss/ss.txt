SiSwati
=======

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/ssw>`__ identifies this language as ``ssw``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* MZ: Mozambique
* ZA: South Africa
* US: United States of America
* ZW: Zimbabwe
* SZ: Eswatini
