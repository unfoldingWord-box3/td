euskara, euskera
================

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/eus>`__ identifies this language as ``eus``.

* CL: Chile
* FR: France
* VE: Venezuela (Bolivarian Republic of)
* ES: Spain
* US: United States of America
