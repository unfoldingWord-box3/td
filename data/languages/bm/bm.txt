Bamanankan
----------

.. note:: The `Ethnologue <https://www.ethnologue.com/language/bam>`__ identifies this language as ``bam``.

This language is spoken in the following countries:

* BF: Burkina Faso
* GM: Gambia
* ML: Mali
* MR: Mauritania
* NE: Niger
* SN: Senegal
* US: United States of America
* CA: Canada
* GN: Guinea
